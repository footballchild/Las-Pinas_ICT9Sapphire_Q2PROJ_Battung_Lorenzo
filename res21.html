Mini Pots

Here's a playful recipe for Mini Pots that could look similar to Fortnite's health potions!

Mini Potion Jars (Health-Potion-Inspired Treats) These Mini Potion Jars are small, colorful, and refreshing jelly-like treats. They're inspired by Fortniteâ€™s Mini Pots that restore health in the game. You can make them as individual servings in small jars, and use fruit-flavored gelatin to make them look like colorful potions!
Ingredients:
2 packets (3 oz each) of flavored gelatin (Jell-O) in different colors (like blue raspberry, strawberry, or lime)
1 can of coconut milk (optional, for a creamy layer)
1/2 cup boiling water (for each packet of gelatin)
1/2 cup cold water (for each packet of gelatin)
Mini jars or small glass containers (or small plastic cups)
Optional garnish: Whipped cream or edible glitter for extra magic!

Instructions:
1. Prepare the First Layer (for your first "potion" color): Boil 1/2 cup of water and pour it into a bowl. Add the flavored gelatin (e.g., blue raspberry) to the hot water, stirring until the gelatin dissolves completely. Add 1/2 cup of cold water to the mixture and stir to combine. Pour the gelatin mixture into small jars or containers, filling each jar about halfway.
2. Place the jars in the fridge and allow the first layer to set for 1-2 hours, or until firm. Prepare the Second Layer (for a contrasting "potion" color):
3. In a separate bowl, repeat the process with another flavor of gelatin (e.g., strawberry or lime).
4.If you want to add a creamy layer (to mimic the potion effect), you can use coconut milk instead of water for the second layer. Mix the coconut milk with the gelatin mixture. After the first layer is fully set, pour the second gelatin mixture on top of the first layer. Again, fill the jars about halfway or as desired. Refrigerate again for another 1-2 hours until set. Assemble the Mini Pots:
5.Once both layers are set, your "Mini Pots" should be colorful and fun! You can use a spoon to lightly swirl the edges for a mystical effect. Optional Garnish:
6.If you want to add a bit of fun, you can top the Mini Pots with whipped cream and a sprinkle of edible glitter to make them look even more like magical health potions. You can also use small straws or colorful spoons for serving!
Serve and Enjoy: Serve your Mini Potion Jars chilled as a fun treat for a Fortnite-themed party or just as a playful nod to the game. These Mini Potion Jars are a fun, vibrant, and sweet way to recreate the feeling of Fortnite's Mini Pots in the real world. Enjoy!
